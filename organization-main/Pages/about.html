About Us

With the sole aim of promoting and facilitating the education of ALL Sri Lankan children, regardless of their gender, creed, socioeconomic status, or any other bias, we launched Helping Hands in 2020. We sincerely believe that education is one of the most empowering tools a child can possess and is a significant factor in predicting their success in the future. We have witnessed first-hand how it can break down barriers, raise people out of poverty, and have transforming potential.
